My name is Nguyễn Đình Tiến. I was born in Huế and raised in Bình Dương. I lived in Sài Gòn for a while before moving to Hà Nội. The majority of my friends believe that I speak with a mixture of Southern and Northern accents, though my active vocabulary spans all three regions.

I am a PhD student in the QCMX group hosted at École polytechnique. We are working on developing a new kind of superconducting qubit with built-in topological protection at the hardware level.

I was part of Hung’s group where I studied control of superconducting transmon qudits. I briefly ventured into the realm of bosonic quantum error correction as a visiting student in the group of Valerio Scarani and Ng Hui Khoon.
