Valerio (CQT) paid a visit to Hung’s group

Newer: The Pentatonic Scale in Guitar
Older: Just another normal meeting at HPC

Prof. Valerio Scarani arrived in Hanoi on 9th October, 2022 following an invitation from our PI, Dr Hung-Quoc Nguyen. We had a nice informal seminar discussing undergoing projects the following day. Below are some memorable moments from the event.

Picture 1. Presenting my work. Prof. Valerio did some experimental work with NMR back when he was a postdoc, so we've had somewhat common issues with the overlapping spectra of different subspace transitions. Picture 2. Ha presenting his work on realization of neutrino oscillations on quantum computers. Picture 3. The group and Prof. Valerio discussing some of the previous results. Though focusing on foundational aspects of quantum mechanics, he's still a pretty sharp guy when it comes to technology stuff. Picture 4. Our PI, Hung, discussing some of his recent ideas on achieving a new nonlocality state using the quantum circuit model--his work with a former student named Duc Tran (who's now in France pursuing his Masters). The duo had a great time I assume, since Prof. Valerio's expertise is the Bell inequality and Hardy's paradox. Picture 5. Himself expanding more on the violation of Bell inequality from a geometrical perspective. The cube thing is the Hamming cube (or at least my best guess 😀). Picture 6. The group and Prof. Valerio. P/s: The day culminated with a lovely dinner with Mrs Nhung as a special guest.
